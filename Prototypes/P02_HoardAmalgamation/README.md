# P02 — Hoard Amalgamation

**Design Question:** Can a room-as-mob encounter feel fun to fight with a vacuum?

**Core Concept:** The entire room IS the enemy — a writhing mass of junk with phases,
weak points, and modifiers that require specific vacuum attachments to counter.

**Success Criteria:**
- Player feels urgency and threat when entering the room
- Peeling back layers is satisfying (not tedious)
- Attachment requirement feels like a puzzle, not a wall
- Phase transitions are dramatic and readable
- Dying to a room full of junk feels funny, not frustrating
